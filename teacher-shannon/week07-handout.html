I.

Today's Riddle

今日謎題
⚾
"I have a diamond, but I am not jewelry. I have a plate, but I have no food. I am massive and loud, but sometimes I am hushed. I get very gritty when things are intense. What am I?"
提示：粗體字都是這週要學的英文字。Diamond 與 plate 在棒球裡有特別的意思！
⚾ A Baseball Stadium
棒球場 · 球場
In baseball, the diamond is the infield shape (內野菱形); the plate is home plate (本壘). Stadiums are massive, sometimes hushed before a pitch, and gritty (沙土覆蓋的) on the field.
