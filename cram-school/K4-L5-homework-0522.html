📚 K4 Lesson 5 作業解析

✏️ 填充一：中翻英填空（第 1–11 題）
1
地震在 2025 年 3 月 28 日襲擊了這個城市。
The ______ ______ the city on March 28th, 2025.
答案：earthquake  /  hit
解析：「地震」= earthquake（n.），「襲擊」= hit（v.）。句子時態為過去式，hit 本身就是過去式（hit–hit–hit 三態同形）。
2
你應該要緊緊抓住某個東西直到搖晃停止。
You should ______ ______ to something until the shaking stops.
答案：hold  /  on
解析：「緊緊抓住」= hold on to + something。hold on = 抓緊、堅持；后接 to 表示抓住某物。
3
Eva 感受到地板上下搖晃 / 很害怕，躲在桌子下面，遮蓋她的頭。
I felt the floor ______ up and down when I slept last night.
I was ______, so I ______ under the table and ______ my head with my hands.
答案：shaking  /  scared  /  hid  /  covered
解析：
• 感官動詞 feel + O + V-ing（強調動作正在發生），所以填 shaking。
• scared = 感到害怕（adj.）；be scared。
• hide（躲藏）過去式 = hid。
• cover（遮蓋）過去式 = covered。
4
你有聽到某人在外面大叫嗎？ / 他從樹上掉下來且傷到他自己。
Did you ______ ______ shouting outside this morning?
He ______ ______ the tree and hurt ______.
答案：hear  /  someone  /  fell  /  from  /  himself
解析：
• hear someone + V-ing（聽到某人在大叫）= hear someone shouting。
• fall from...（從...掉落），fall 過去式 = fell。
• hurt himself：傷到「他自己」→ 反身代名詞 himself（he → himself）。
5
一個女孩跌入那條很深的河裡 / 那個男人正試著要自己幫助她 / 那個女孩正緊緊抓住他的手。
A girl just ______ into the ______ river. Let's help her.
The man is trying to help her by ______. And the girl is ______ ______ to his hands.
答案：fell  /  deep  /  himself  /  holding  /  on
解析：
• fall into = 跌入（fall 過去式 = fell）。
• deep（深的）形容詞修飾 river。
• by himself = 靠他自己（= alone）。
• hold on to = 緊緊抓住，現在進行式 is holding on to。
6
在地震發生的時候搭電梯是很危險的。那個時刻我們應該怎麼做？
It is ______ to take an ______ when an ______ happens.
Then what should we do at that ______?
答案：dangerous  /  elevator  /  earthquake  /  moment
解析：
• It is + adj. + to V... = 做...是...的（虛主詞 It）。
• dangerous（危險的），elevator（電梯），earthquake（地震），moment（時刻）。
• at that moment = 在那個時刻。
7
颱風「木蘭」將於明天登陸。你們自己應該做好準備，否則會相當危險。
______ Mulan will hit ______ tomorrow. You should prepare ______, or it will be very ______.
答案：Typhoon  /  land  /  yourselves  /  dangerous
解析：
• Typhoon（颱風）是專有名詞類別名稱，放在名字前面。
• hit land = 登陸（land 在此為名詞「陸地」）。
• 「你們自己」= yourselves（you 複數的反身代名詞）。
• or = 否則（otherwise）；引出否定結果。
8
地板不僅前後而且上下搖晃 / 那聽起來好嚇人 / 幸運地，沒有嚴重的事情發生。
The floor was shaking not ______ back and forth ______ also up and down when the ______ hit the city.
That sounds ______. — Luckily, ______ serious happened.
答案：only  /  but  /  earthquake  /  scary  /  nothing
解析：
• not only... but also...（不僅...而且...）= 連接兩個並列動作或方向。
• scary = 嚇人的、令人害怕的（形容詞）。
• nothing serious happened = 沒有嚴重的事情發生。
9
拉開那個抽屜，你會看到一些餅乾跟蠟燭在裡面。
______ that ______, and you will see some ______ and ______ inside.
答案：Pull open  /  drawer  /  cookies  /  candles
解析：
• pull open = 拉開（動詞片語）；句型是祈使句（命令句）。
• drawer（抽屜），cookies（餅乾），candles（蠟燭）。
• 「..., and you will...」= 祈使句 + and + 結果句。
10
這颱風給了我們一個教訓 / 一間公寓倒塌了 / 不管任何時候，都不想再次經歷了。
The ______ taught us a ______.
An ______ fell down. I don't want to experience it ______ again.
答案：typhoon  /  lesson  /  apartment  /  anytime (/ ever)
解析：
• teach someone a lesson = 給某人一個教訓（teach 過去式 = taught）。
• apartment（公寓）。
• experience（動詞）= 經歷；anytime / ever = 任何時候。
11
我聽到窗戶碎裂了。讓我去查看一下。/ 待在家裡。如果你去外面，將會很危險。
I ______ the ______ ______. Let me check it out.
Stay at home. It will be very ______ if you go outside.
答案：heard  /  window  /  breaking (/ crack)  /  dangerous
解析：
• 感官動詞 hear + O + V-ing（強調正在發生）= heard the window breaking（碎裂的過程）。
• 也可用 crack（碎裂一聲），視題目空格數量而定。
• dangerous（危險的）。
✏️ 填充二：動詞形式 & 反身代名詞（第 12–19 題）
12
填入正確的動詞形式
① I heard my mother ask my brother ______ (clean) his room last night.
② We saw a woman ______ (cry) in the park.
③ Erica watches her mom ______ (make) dinner in the kitchen every evening.
答案：① to clean  ② cry / crying  ③ make
解析：
• ① ask someone to V（請/叫某人做…），不是感官動詞，要用 to V。
• ② see + O + V / V-ing（裸不定詞或現在分詞皆可）。
• ③ watch + O + V（感官動詞後接裸不定詞）。
13
填入正確的反身代名詞
① Cathy made the cake for me all by ______. It was very nice of her.
② The man is strange. He talks to ______ in the park almost every day.
③ Bruce and Michael enjoyed ______ at the beach last weekend.
答案：① herself  ② himself  ③ themselves
解析：
• Cathy → she → herself。
• The man → he → himself。
• Bruce and Michael → they → themselves（enjoy oneself = 玩得開心）。
14
填入正確的反身代名詞
① My cat cleans ______ every day.
② Mom and Dad, please take care of ______ when I am not home.
③ My five-year-old son can put on clothes all by ______.
答案：① itself  ② yourselves  ③ himself
解析：
• My cat（動物，非特定性別）→ itself。
• Mom and Dad（= you 複數）→ yourselves。
• My son → he → himself（by himself = 獨自地）。
15
填入正確的動詞形式
① Do you feel the classroom ______ (shake)?
② We saw some people ______ (dance) in the park.
③ The teacher told the students ______ (not talk) to each other in class.
答案：① shake / shaking  ② dance / dancing  ③ not to talk
解析：
• ① feel + O + V / V-ing（感官動詞）。
• ② see + O + V / V-ing（感官動詞）。
• ③ tell + O + not to V（tell 不是感官動詞，後接不定詞；否定加 not）。
16
填入正確的動詞形式
① Tom listened to his daughter ______ (tell) a story.
② Murry saw the bad guy ______ (follow) a woman.
③ Jenny heard a woman ______ (cry) out for help.
答案：① tell  ② follow / following  ③ cry / crying
解析：
• listen to + O + V / V-ing（感官動詞）。
• see + O + V / V-ing。
• hear + O + V / V-ing。
17
填入正確的動詞形式
① Don't make any noise. I'm listening to Terry ______ (sing) on TV.
② Adam saw Mark and Linda ______ (eat) lunch together yesterday.
③ Earthquake! I felt the floor ______ (shake).
答案：① sing / singing  ② eat / eating  ③ shake / shaking
解析：
• 以上皆為感官動詞（listen to、see、feel）+ O + V 或 V-ing 的結構。
• V-ing 強調動作「進行中」；V（裸不定詞）強調動作「完整發生」。
18
填入正確的反身代名詞
① What happened to John? I saw him talking to ______ in the classroom.
② My husband was not home yesterday, so I cleaned the house by ______.
③ Boys, just do all the homework by ______.
答案：① himself  ② myself  ③ yourselves
解析：
• John / him → himself。
• I → myself。
• Boys（= you 複數）→ yourselves（by oneself = 靠自己）。
19
選填正確的動詞形式
① I heard a man ______ (to sing / sings / sing) a song to a woman in the park yesterday.
② Wendy just felt raindrops ______ (to fall / falling / falls) on her head.
③ Tommy and Amy listened to their aunt ______ (told / to tell / tell) stories.
④ Jill only saw one fish ______ (swims / swim / to swim) in the river.
答案：① sing  ② falling  ③ tell  ④ swim
解析：
• ① heard + O + V（完整發生的動作，所以用裸不定詞 sing）。
• ② felt + O + V-ing（雨滴正在落下，強調進行，所以用 falling）。
• ③ listen to + O + V（裸不定詞，tell）。
• ④ saw + O + V（裸不定詞，swim）。
✏️ 填充三：not only... but also... 合併句子（第 20–22, 28 題）
20
Ms. Thompson is an actress. Ms. Thompson is a writer. → 合併
答案：Ms. Thompson is not only an actress but also a writer.
解析：主詞相同，be 動詞後的名詞用 not only... but also... 連接兩個補語。
21
My coat is comfortable. My coat is fashionable. → 合併
答案：My coat is not only comfortable but also fashionable.
解析：主詞相同（My coat），be 動詞後的形容詞用 not only... but also... 連接。
22
My father made me breakfast. My father drove me to school. → 合併
答案：My father not only made me breakfast but also drove me to school.
解析：主詞相同（My father），兩個動詞片語（made / drove）用 not only... but also... 連接。注意兩個動詞都要過去式。
28
I play soccer well. My brother plays soccer well. → 合併
答案：Not only I but also my brother plays soccer well.
解析：「Not only A but also B + 動詞」→ 動詞與最近的主詞（B = my brother，第三人稱單數）一致，所以用 plays。
✏️ 填充四：反身代名詞綜合練習（第 23–24 題）
23
填入適當的反身代名詞
① My mom and dad will be away for two days, so my brother and I need to take care of ______.
② The door opens by ______ when someone gets close.
③ The girl is only three years old. She can't take a bath by ______.
④ Boys, help ______ to some ice cream. It is on the dining table.
⑤ Ann: It was dangerous for you to go swimming by ______. Never do that again.
⑥ I heard John talking to ______ in the room.
答案：① ourselves  ② itself  ③ herself  ④ yourselves  ⑤ yourself  ⑥ himself
解析：
• ① my brother and I = we → ourselves（take care of oneself = 照顧自己）。
• ② The door（物）→ itself（by itself = 自動地）。
• ③ The girl → she → herself（by herself = 獨自）。
• ④ Boys（= you 複數）→ yourselves（help yourself to sth. = 請自便）。
• ⑤ you（單數的 Ben）→ yourself。
• ⑥ John → he → himself（自言自語）。
24
John made himself a cup of coffee this morning. → 主詞改為 John and Mary
答案：John and Mary made themselves a cup of coffee this morning.
解析：John and Mary = they（複數）→ 反身代名詞改為 themselves。動詞也維持過去式 made。
✏️ 填充五：感官動詞合併句（第 25–27 題）
25
Someone played the guitar in the classroom. That person also sang a beautiful song. → 用 Peter heard... 開頭
答案：Peter heard someone play the guitar and sing a beautiful song in the classroom.
解析：hear + O + V（裸不定詞）；兩個動作（play、sing）以 and 連接，共用同一個受詞 someone。
26
I saw my brother in the park. He was playing basketball with his friends. → 用 saw 合併
答案：I saw my brother playing basketball with his friends in the park.
解析：see + O + V-ing（強調「看到他正在進行」的狀態）= saw my brother playing...。也可用裸不定詞 play，但 playing 更能強調「正在發生」。
27
你的經歷聽起來就像電影場景一樣！/ 我不想再次成為真實生活中地震的主角了。
Ella：Your experience sounds like a ______ from a movie!
Ben：Well… actually, I don't want to be the ______ actor in a ______ ______ ______ again.
答案：scene  /  main  /  real  /  life  /  earthquake
解析：
• a scene from a movie = 電影中的場景。
• the main actor = 主要演員（主角）。
• a real life earthquake = 真實生活中的地震（real life 當形容詞修飾 earthquake）。
