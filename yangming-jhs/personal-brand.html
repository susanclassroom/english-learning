I.

A Personal-Branding Morning — and a Cultural Showcase

個人品牌講座暨文化展演
November 10, 2025 · 2025 年 11 月 10 日 · Whole school assembly · 全校參與

Two international guest speakers, a school-wide talk on personal branding, and a student-led cultural showcase that ran from harmonica music to martial arts.

兩位國際講者、一場關於「個人品牌」的講座、再加上一場由學生主導、從口琴到武術的文化展演——這是陽明國中 2025 年 11 月 10 日的早晨。

II.

The Day's Story

這一天的紀事

Two Guests, One Question 兩位講者，一個問題

On November 10, 2025, Yangming Junior High welcomed two international guests — Leon from Canada and David from the United States — for a school-wide morning on "personal branding." Their talk pressed students to take seriously a question that adults often delay too long: What makes you, you?

2025 年 11 月 10 日，陽明國中迎來兩位國際講者——來自加拿大的 Leon 與來自美國的 David，於全校早會中以「個人品牌」為題進行專題分享。他們希望學生認真面對一個成年人常常拖延太久的問題：是什麼讓你成為你自己？

Their advice was deceptively simple. Start with self-awareness. Find your strengths, find what you love, and be honest about both. Then build the habit that everyone agreed mattered most: write something down every day. Not for grades, not for show — just to listen to your own thinking out loud.

他們給的建議聽起來簡單，做起來不容易：從「自我覺察」開始。找到自己的優勢、找到自己真正熱愛的事，並對這兩件事都誠實。接著養成大家都同意最重要的習慣——每天把想法寫下來。不是為了分數，不是為了表演，只是為了聽見自己真正在想什麼。

"Personal branding starts the moment you stop performing and start listening to yourself."

「個人品牌的起點，是停止表演、開始傾聽自己。」

The Showcase 學生展演

After the talk, the floor was the students'. Yangming's harmonica players opened with a piece full of warmth and precision. A second group introduced Changhua's local sights and food in English — from the Bagua Mountain Buddha to nuomi-changchang rice sausages — turning the city we all share into a story international guests could carry home. The morning closed with a martial-arts demonstration that needed no translation: focus, restraint, and discipline visible in every movement.

講座之後，舞台屬於學生。陽明的口琴隊以一首充滿溫度與精準度的演奏開場。另一組學生用英文介紹彰化的景點與美食——從八卦山大佛到糯米腸——把我們每天身處的城市，變成可以讓國際訪客帶回家的故事。早晨以一場武術展演結尾，那是不需要翻譯的語言：每一個動作都看得到專注、節制與紀律。

What It Meant 這場活動的意義

It would have been easy to make this a one-way lecture. Yangming chose not to. By following the speakers' question with the students' own performance, the school showed that personal branding is not an idea to be received — it is something a community already practices when its young people are given a stage to be themselves on.

這場活動本可以是一場單向的講座。陽明選擇不這樣做。在講者提出問題之後，學校讓學生以自己的演出回應——這樣的安排在告訴大家：個人品牌 不是一個被動接收的概念，而是一個社群在年輕人有舞台、能做自己時，早就在實踐的事。
