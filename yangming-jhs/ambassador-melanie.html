一間跨太平洋的教室

The Class That Travelled 那堂跨海上課的課

For one full season at Yangming, the classroom did not stop at the city limits of Changhua. Through the school's Diplomatic Little Ambassadors program and a partnership with My Culture Connect, our students sat down each week with Teacher Melanie — logging in from New York City — for a series of bilingual lessons that were equal parts language practice and global conversation.

在那一整季的時間裡，陽明的教室並未止於彰化市的邊界。透過學校的「外交小尖兵」課程，以及與人師教育協會的合作，學生每週與來自紐約的 Melanie 老師視訊上課——一系列既是英語練習、也是世界對話的雙語課程。

Melanie taught with the kind of patience that lets a quiet thirteen-year-old find their voice without rushing them. By the second month, students were asking unprompted questions; by the end, they were taking turns running the discussion. The change was in their English, yes — but more visibly in their willingness to speak.

Melanie 老師的耐心很特別——讓一位安靜的十三歲學生，不必趕、慢慢找到自己的聲音。第二個月起，學生開始主動發問；學期結束前，他們已經輪流主持討論。改變當然在英文表達上，但更明顯的，是他們願意開口這件事。

"Yangming is dedicated to bringing international education to our students. The course is finished — but we will keep exploring the world together."

「陽明國中致力於發展多元的國際教育——這門課結束了，但我們會繼續和世界一起對話。」

Topics That Stretched Them 兩個讓孩子深思的主題

Topic 1
Sustainable Development
永續發展

Lessons on what "sustainable" really means — from climate basics to the everyday choices that scale up across a generation.

Topic 2
Critical Thinking
批判性思考

How to read a claim, ask why, and form your own view. The hardest skill for any age — and the most useful one Melanie left behind.

Behind the Scenes · 幕後團隊
A Quiet Team Made It Possible
這些老師讓每一堂視訊課都順利進行

Cross-time-zone classes don't run themselves. Yangming wishes to thank Principal Chang Yao-Chung for steering the program; Director Huang Ling-Mei (黃玲玫組長, Curriculum Office) and Director Chen Bing-Chang (陳炳彰組長, IT Office) for the planning and the technology; and teachers Liu Pei-Rou (劉沛柔老師) and Chiang Hung-Hsuan (江虹萱老師) for the patient afternoon-by-afternoon support that made every session work.

感謝張耀忠校長的指導與規劃，感謝教務處黃玲玫組長、資訊組陳炳彰組長，以及劉沛柔老師、江虹萱老師在幕後辛苦的技術支援與陪伴，讓每堂視訊課都能順利進行。

What Comes Next 下一步

The course is finished, but the door is not. Yangming and My Culture Connect are already planning the next round of international programming — new teachers, new themes, and the same commitment to giving every Yangming student a stage that reaches beyond Changhua.

這門課結束了，但門沒有關。陽明國中與人師教育協會已經在規劃下一輪的國際課程——新的老師、新的主題，以及同一個信念：讓每一位陽明孩子，都有一個能延伸到彰化以外的舞台。
