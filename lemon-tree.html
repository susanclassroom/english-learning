💬 Discussion  討論時間
  • 1
    Have you ever felt bored or lonely on a Sunday afternoon? What did you do?
    你有沒有曾在週日午後感到無聊或寂寞？你做了什麼？
  • 2
    The singer keeps seeing a "yellow lemon-tree" instead of the "blue sky." What do you think the lemon-tree represents?
    歌手一直看到「黃色的檸檬樹」而非「藍天」，你認為檸檬樹象徵什麼？
  • 3
    The song says "Isolation is not good for me." Do you agree? Why or why not?
    歌曲說「孤獨對我不好」，你同意嗎？為什麼？
  • 4
    What would you do to escape a boring, rainy Sunday? Write 2–3 sentences!
    如果你遇到無聊的下雨天週日，你會怎麼做？寫 2–3 句話！
