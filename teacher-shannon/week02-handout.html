The Daily Grind
日常磨練 · 陽明外交小尖兵
I.

What is "The Daily Grind"?

什麼叫做「每日磨練」？
"The Daily Grind is the hard work and repetitive routine you do every day to reach your goals." 「每日磨練」（the daily grind）就是為了達成目標，每天都得認真重複做的那些苦工。
II.

The Power Riddle

能量謎題 · 你覺得這是什麼？
🔍
"I always start the morning at 100%, but I usually end the day at 0%. I never get hungry for a snack, but sometimes I need a boost. If I crash, the vibe is not going to be good. What am I?"
提示：注意粗體字 — 這些都是這週的核心英文單字。
📱 A Phone Battery
手機電池
Phones start the day fully charged at 100% and drain to 0% — they don't eat snacks but sometimes need a "boost" (charge), and when they "crash", the whole "vibe" of your day is ruined.
III.

Core 5 Vocabulary

本週 5 大核心單字 · 點 🔊 聽發音
⏰
Deadline
/ˈdɛd.laɪn/ · n.
截止時間 · 死線
The exact time you must finish your work.
"My homework deadline is Friday at 5 PM."
⚙️
Grind
/ɡraɪnd/ · n. (also v.)
苦工 · 重複辛苦的工作
Hard, repetitive work — like a long school day.
"School is a grind, but I love learning."
🍪
Snack
/snæk/ · n. / v.
點心 · 零食 · 吃零食
A little bit of food for quick energy.
"I had a snack between classes."
💀
Crash
/kræʃ/ · v.
崩潰睡著 · 體力歸零
To suddenly fall asleep because you are at 0% energy.
"After the test, I went home and crashed."
✨
Vibe
/vaɪb/ · n.
氛圍 · 感覺
The mood or feeling of a time.
"I love the weekend vibes."

V.

My Daily Formula

每日造句公式 · 三條尺度合一
I [Frequency] feel [Power Level] during/when [Grind Word].
挑戰看看：每一句都用上頻率字 + 能量字 + 本週 5 單字之一
I always feel exhausted when I have a deadline. 每次有死線，我都覺得累爆。
I sometimes feel energized when I grab a snack. 有時候吃個零食就覺得活力滿滿。
My Friday vibe is pumped because I feel chill after the school grind. 週五的氛圍超嗨，因為一週苦工結束後我終於可以放鬆。
