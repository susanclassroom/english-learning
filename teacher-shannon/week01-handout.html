III.

Grammar Focus

文法核心 · I am vs. I have
I am
Identity / 身份
For who you are, your age, your feeling.
用來描述你「是誰」「幾歲」「感覺如何」
I am + Name / Age / Adjective
"I am Mary."
"I am 13 years old."
"I am in Junior High."
"I am happy today."
I have
Possession / 擁有
For things or people that belong to you.
用來描述你「擁有」的人事物
I have + Noun / Object
"I have a brother."
"I have a grandmother."
"I have a dog at home."
"I have a piano lesson today."
IV.

All About Me

自我介紹 · 8 大問句與範例答案
01
What is your name?
你叫什麼名字？
My name is ______________.填上你的英文名字或中文拼音
02
How old are you?
你幾歲？
I am ______ years old.用 "I am" 描述年齡
03
Who is in your family?
你家有誰？
My family has ____ people. I have ______________.例如：a mother, father, 1 brother, a sister
04
Do you have pets at home?
你家有寵物嗎？
Yes, I have ______________. — or — No, I do not have any pets.有寵物用 "I have"，沒有用 "I do not have"
05
What is your favorite food?
你最愛吃什麼？
My favorite food is ______________.
06
What is a hobby you enjoy?
你有什麼喜歡的興趣？
I enjoy ______________.例：playing piano · playing badminton · exercising · painting · playing video games
07
What is your best subject in school?
學校裡你最強的科目是？
My best subject in school is ______________.例：math · history · art
08
What is your talent?
你的才能是什麼？
My talent is ______________.例：playing music · math · swimming
V.

Game: 2 Truths & 1 Lie

破冰遊戲 · 兩真一假
Icebreaker · 破冰遊戲
Tell us three things — but one is a lie!
說出三件關於自己的事，但其中一件是假的！
Each student writes 3 sentences about themselves: 2 are true, and 1 is a lie. The class (or Teacher Shannon) tries to guess which one is the lie. 每位同學寫下 3 個關於自己的句子：2 句是真的，1 句是假的。同學或 Shannon 老師會猜哪一句是假的。
Example · 範例
Truth ✓ — I have a sister and a dog.
Truth ✓ — My favorite food is dumplings.
Lie ✗ — I am 18 years old.
