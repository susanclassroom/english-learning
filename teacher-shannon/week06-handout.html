I.

Today's Riddle

今日謎題
🗺️
"I have cities, but no houses. I have mountains, but no trees. I have water, but no fish. I have roads, but no cars. What am I?"
🗺️ A Map
地圖
A map shows cities, mountains, water, and roads — but they're all just drawings, not the real things. The riddle plays on this: it has all the labels but none of the stuff.
II.

Vocab A · Travel Basics

7 個旅行必備字 · 含造句起手式
01
Destination
目的地
The place you are going.
"Our destination is [Country / City]."
02
Transport
交通方式
How you travel — plane, train, bus, boat, etc.
"We will take a [plane / train] for transport."
03
Itinerary
行程表 · 每日計畫
The daily plan or schedule for your trip.
"On our itinerary, first we will visit…"
04
Essentials
必備物品 · 一定要帶
Something you must pack — can't travel without.
"My [passport / phone] is essential."
05
Landmark
地標 · 名建築
A famous building or place — easy to recognize.
"We want to see the [Eiffel Tower] landmark."
06
Budget
預算 · 可花的錢
The amount of money you have to spend.
"Our budget for this trip is $157,500 NTD."
07
Attraction
景點 · 觀光地
A fun place that pulls in tourists.
"The most famous attraction is [Disney]."

IV.

Plan Your Dream Trip

用上面 14 個字規劃自己的旅行
Imagine you have a budget of
💰 $150,000 NTD
to travel anywhere you want — use the vocabulary from sections II + III to plan it!
📍
Destination — Where will you go?
你想去哪裡？國家、城市？
✈️
Transport — How will you get there?
你要怎麼過去？飛機、火車、船？
📅
Itinerary — What will you do each day?
每天的行程是什麼？要看哪些地標？
🎯
Landmarks & Attractions — What do you want to see?
必看的地標與景點？
🎒
Essentials — What must you pack?
護照？手機？相機？絕對不能少的是？
💭
Wish list & vibes — Will it be lively or serene? Are there any hidden gems?
