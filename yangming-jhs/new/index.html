I.

About Yangming

關於陽明 · 一所立於彰化市中心的傳統名校
A school where every child grows in confidence — at the heart of Changhua City for over eight decades.
一所立於彰化市中心、以「讓孩子深具信心」為信念的傳統名校。

Welcome to Yangming Junior High School, an institution with over 80 years of history located right in the heart of Changhua City. Our students come from families that highly value education, and for decades our school has been known for its outstanding performance in both academics and character development.

陽明國中是一所位於彰化市中心、超過八十年歷史的傳統名校。學生多來自重視教育的家庭，多年來在學科表現與品格涵養上皆有亮眼成績，是家長口中的「彰化市中心國中」。

We believe every student and every teacher should feel a strong sense of pride as a "Yangming person." Our goal is to continue fostering a culture of academic rigor and strong moral character — building on our traditions to create an even brighter future.

我們相信每一位陽明的學生與老師，都應為「陽明人」這個身份深感驕傲。承先啟後，是我們對下一個世代的承諾。

"Education is what makes a child deeply confident."
「教育的意義是讓孩子深具信心。」
—— 校長 張耀忠
II.

Four Concrete Thrusts

校長四大具體主軸 · 張耀忠校長辦學藍圖

Principal Chang Yao-chung's vision rests on a simple guiding line for every Yangming student — "to be at home in both letters and arms; equally at ease in stillness and motion." From this foundation, four concrete thrusts shape our school life:

張耀忠校長的辦學願景以一句話期勉每位陽明學子——「允文允武、動靜皆宜」。在此基礎上，四大具體主軸構成校務經營的方向：

PILLAR 01
🌐
International Education
推動國際教育 · 營造國際視野
Building global perspective through specialised English programs, multicultural exchanges, and our flagship Diplomatic Pioneer initiative — opening windows so rural-county students can walk confidently into the world.
PILLAR 02
🎵
A Campus of Music
營造樂音校園 · 蘊育人文素養
Music as a daily landscape — through our award-winning Chinese orchestra, harmonica ensemble, and arts programs, students grow into rounded humanistic learners with sensitivity and grace.
PILLAR 03
🔬
Scientific Thinking
倡導科學思維 · 展現優質學風
A culture of curiosity, evidence, and rigour. Our 23 consecutive years as Changhua County science fair team champions — and 23 straight years of qualifying for the National Science Fair, bringing home awards every time — are not the goal. They are the proof that scientific thinking is woven into how Yangming students learn.
PILLAR 04
⚡
Sports & Wellness
推展運動風氣 · 強化健全身心
From martial arts to taekwondo, athletics is part of who we are. Sound mind in sound body — sports build the resilience and discipline that make every other pursuit possible.
"To be at home in both letters and arms; equally at ease in stillness and motion."
「允文允武、動靜皆宜」—— 校長對每位陽明學子的期勉。
III.

A Twenty-Three-Year Streak

彰化縣科展 23 連霸 · 全國科展連續 23 年參賽且得獎
Science Fair Streak · 科展連霸
23 Years
Consecutive Team Championships
Changhua County · 彰化縣
彰化縣中小學科展連續 23 年團體冠軍
Every year for more than two decades, Yangming Junior High has taken the team championship at the Changhua County Science Fair — and qualified those winning teams to represent the county at the National Science Fair, where Yangming has placed in the awards every one of those 23 consecutive years. It is the longest streak of its kind in the region, and the most concrete answer to "what does scientific thinking actually look like?"

過去二十多年來，陽明國中每年都奪下彰化縣中小學科展團體冠軍，並代表彰化縣晉級全國中小學科展——連續 23 年都在全國賽場參賽並得獎。這是「科學思維融入學習」最具體的證明。

Lessons

課程精華 · 持續累積中的 ESL 工作坊

Workshop by Teacher Shannon · Shannon 老師

A growing library of ESL lessons — one teacher, one classroom across the Pacific.

Shannon 老師親自設計的 ESL 課程入口 — 講義與測驗，每季持續新增
Each lesson takes a real-world theme — introductions, daily life, night markets, music, the "vibe check," dream vacations, sports, sharing opinions — and pairs a printable handout with an in-browser quiz. New lessons are added each season, so students can read, listen, and check their understanding right after class, week after week. 每堂課抓一個生活主題，從自我介紹、日常作息、夜市探險到分享意見——每課搭配講義與線上測驗，孩子上完課就能立刻複習、自評。課程持續累積，每季都會新增內容。這是陽明外交小尖兵跨太平洋與 Shannon 老師線上對話一整個學期、又一個學期，慢慢長出來的「精華成果」。

V.

Featured Programs

特色課程與社團 · 四大主軸的具體實踐
🌍
Diplomatic Pioneers
外交小尖兵 · 國際教育旗艦
Our flagship international-education program nurtures young diplomats — students learn presentation, intercultural communication, and global affairs in English, representing Yangming in national-level events.
陽明國際教育的旗艦計畫——學生以英文進行簡報、跨文化交流與時事討論，代表陽明出征全國級競賽，是「國際視野」最具體的舞台。
🎼
Chinese Orchestra & Harmonica Ensemble
國樂團 · 口琴隊
Yangming's Chinese orchestra and harmonica ensemble are perennial winners at national music competitions — daily rehearsals turning an ordinary campus into one filled with music.
陽明國樂團與口琴隊長年於全國音樂比賽獲獎——日常的練習與展演，把整座校園變成一首流動的樂章。
🏆 全國音樂比賽常勝軍
🥋
Martial Arts & Taekwondo
武術 · 跆拳道
Discipline through movement. Our martial arts and taekwondo teams give students a path to physical excellence that carries over into focus, resilience, and self-respect.
武術與跆拳道專長隊，讓孩子在體能訓練中建立紀律、韌性與自尊——「動」的修練是「靜」的根基。
🎓
Gifted Student Programs
資優教育 · 適性發展
A dedicated team of teachers nurtures gifted students while also providing targeted learning support — ensuring every child has the resources they need to succeed.
資優教育與適性學習並重——既栽培資優生走得更遠，也讓每位需要支持的孩子被穩穩接住。
💻
Digital Learning
數位學習 · 科技自學
From online platforms to self-directed digital projects, Yangming students learn to navigate technology with both skill and judgement — preparing them for the lifelong learning ahead.
從線上平台到自主探索專題，孩子學會用科技解決真實問題，為終身學習打下基礎。
"There is no miracle in life — only the trace of unceasing effort."
「生命沒有奇蹟，只有不斷努力的痕跡。」
—— 期許在陽明每一項的「存在」都成為傳奇，每一段成長「路程」都充滿美好回憶。
VI.

Our Story

校史時間軸 · 從家政女子學校到陽明國中
1940
Founded as Changhua Municipal Home Economics Girls' School
創立於民國 29 年 4 月，初名「彰化市立彰化家政女子學校」。
1943
New campus completed and occupied
民國 32 年新校舍落成，遷入今址（彰化市長順街 76 號）。
1945
Reorganised as Girls' Junior Commercial Vocational School
日本投降後，改制為「彰化市立女子初級商業職業學校」。
1954
Senior division added; renamed Girls' Commercial Vocational School
民國 43 年增設高商部，更名為「彰化縣立女子商業職業學校」。
1968
Converted to Yangming Junior High School with the launch of nine-year compulsory education
民國 57 年配合九年國民義務教育，改制為「彰化縣立陽明國民中學」，沿用至今。
1976
Supplementary evening school program established
民國 65 年增設補校。
1997
Yangming Hall multi-purpose complex completed
民國 86 年陽明館完工——含行政、電腦教學、學生活動與游泳設施的多功能大樓。
Today
Principal Chang Yao-chung (張耀忠) leads the four-pillar vision
現任校長張耀忠以「允文允武、動靜皆宜」為核心，推動四大具體主軸：國際教育、樂音校園、科學思維、運動風氣。
