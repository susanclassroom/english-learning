I.

About Sigang

關於西港 · 一所沿海的小校
A small school at the very edge of the Changhua Plain, where the Zhuoshui River meets the Taiwan Strait.
一所位於彰化平原最西南端、濁水溪入海口的小學。我們離海很近，離風更近。

Founded in 1930, Sigang Elementary serves five villages of Dacheng Township — Sigang, Donggang, Gongguan, Taixi, and Dingzhuang. We are a school small enough that every child is known by name, and big enough to dream of the world.

西港創校於 1930 年，服務大城鄉的五個村——西港、東港、公館、台西、頂莊。是一所小到每個孩子都被叫得出名字，又大到敢做世界級夢想的學校。

The land here is shaped by salt wind, low fields, and the long horizon of the Taiwan Strait. The pace of life is slow, the agriculture is tied to the seasons, and the sea is never out of sight.

這裡的土地由鹹風、低田與綿延的海岸線塑造。生活步調緩慢，農作依四季而行，海，從來不離視線。

"Life is Good!"
校訓——希望孩子們學會的，不是分數，而是熱愛生活的方式。

III.

Featured Programs

特色課程 · 把在地生活變成學習
🌾
Food & Agriculture Education
食農教育
From local crops to the family table — students learn how food, land, and community are woven together.
從在地農作出發，連結食物、土地與社區，讓孩子認識自己餐桌上的故事。
🌊
Marine Environmental Education
海洋環保課程
With the Taiwan Strait at our doorstep, our students learn what it means to "live facing the sea" — coastal ecology, conservation, and beach stewardship.
大城鄉沿海地理優勢——孩子在西海岸學「面海的生活」，了解海洋生態、保育與淨灘行動。
🎨
Resident Artist Program
駐校藝術家 · 王莉老師
Artist Wang Li serves as our resident artist, bringing creative practice into everyday classroom life.
王莉老師為西港駐校藝術家，把藝術創作帶入日常的課堂與校園。
🤝
Erhlin Community College Partnership
與二林社區大學合作
A long-running partnership with Erhlin Community College brings ecology educators and community resources into the classroom.
與二林社區大學長期合作，將生態認識教學與在地社群資源帶進校園。
🧸
Affiliated Preschool · Since 1987
附設幼兒園 · 39 年歷史
Established in 1987 (formally re-designated in 2012), our affiliated preschool has nurtured generations of Sigang children before they enter elementary school.
自 1987 年設立、2012 年改制為附設幼兒園，39 年來陪伴一代又一代西港孩子踏上學習旅程。
IV.

Our Story

校史時間軸 · 風雨九十六年
1930
Founded as Dacheng Public School Sigang Branch
創校之初為「大城公學校西港分教場」。
1941
Became an independent school: Sigang National School
脫離大城公學校，獨立為「西港國民學校」。
1968
Renamed Sigang Elementary School with the launch of nine-year compulsory education
配合九年國民義務教育，更名為「西港國民小學」，沿用至今。
1987
Affiliated kindergarten established
設立附設幼稚園，後於 2012 年依法改制為附設幼兒園。
2021
Welcomed students from Dingzhuang Elementary after its closure
大城鄉頂莊國民小學廢校，學生轉入本校。
2024
Principal Chuang Wen-kuei (莊文魁) takes office
莊文魁校長於 2024 年 8 月接任，承接前任洪信德校長 2016–2024 年的耕耘。
