I.

The Music
You Hear Down the Hall

陽明的口琴隊與國樂團
School feature · 校園傳統 · Two ensembles · 兩支隊伍

Walk into Yangming on a Tuesday afternoon, and you'll hear two sounds at once: the clean breath of a harmonica ensemble, and the bowed warmth of a Chinese orchestra. Both have been here longer than most of the teachers.

星期二下午走進陽明，你會同時聽見兩種聲音：一股口琴吹奏的乾淨呼吸，和一陣國樂團溫暖的弓弦——兩支隊伍，都比這所學校的多數老師還資深。

II.

Two Ensembles

兩支聲音不同的隊伍
🎵
Harmonica Ensemble
口琴隊

Yangming's harmonica ensemble is one of the school's longest-running music programs — consistent finalists at national music competitions, and the group most likely to be chosen when an international guest comes to visit. It is also the ensemble whose performance opened the November 2025 personal-branding showcase.

陽明的口琴隊是學校歷史最久的音樂團隊之一，多年於全國音樂比賽穩定打進決賽，也是學校招待國際訪客時最常被推上舞台的隊伍。2025 年 11 月的個人品牌講座暨文化展演，開場演出正是口琴隊。

🎻
Chinese Orchestra
國樂團

The Chinese orchestra (guoyue) carries Yangming's traditional-music side — erhu, pipa, guzheng, dizi, and the percussion family. Members rehearse in mixed grades, with 9th-graders mentoring 7th-graders directly through the score — one of the school's quietest but most reliable forms of cross-cohort teaching.

國樂團承擔陽明傳統音樂的這一邊——二胡、琵琶、古箏、笛、以及一整組打擊樂。國樂團採跨年級混合練習，三年級會直接坐在七年級身旁，從譜上手把手帶——這是陽明校內最安靜、卻最穩定的一種跨年級教學。

Why Music Stays at Yangming 音樂為什麼留在陽明？

Music programs at junior-highs across Taiwan have come and gone with funding cycles, principal changes, and shifting curriculum priorities. Yangming's two ensembles have outlasted all of that — partly because the parents in central Changhua have always sent their children expecting that the school still teaches things that cannot be tested, and partly because, year after year, students have taken up the harmonica or the erhu simply because their older siblings did.

在台灣，許多國中的音樂團隊隨經費、校長更換、課綱調整而起伏。陽明的兩支音樂團隊撐過了這一切——一部分是因為彰化市的家長，一直把孩子送來時就帶著「這所學校還會教那些不能用考試測的事情」的期待；一部分是一屆又一屆的學生，會因為哥哥姊姊吹過口琴、拉過二胡，自己也跟著上去——這所學校的音樂，其實是被學生自己接力傳下來的。

You can hear it in November, when the school welcomes international guests; in May, when the music elective showcases its term work; and on quieter afternoons, drifting from a third-floor practice room into the courtyard outside.

你可以在 11 月聽到他們——當學校招待國際訪客時；可以在 5 月聽到他們——當音樂選修發表期末成果時；也可以在比較安靜的下午聽到他們——從三樓練習室飄出來，越過走廊，進入操場。

"At Yangming, music is what the older kid passes to the younger kid before homework starts."

「在陽明，音樂是寫作業之前，由大孩子交給小孩子的東西。」
