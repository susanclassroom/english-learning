III.

The "Counting" Rule

可數 vs 不可數名詞
How Many?
Countable
Things you can count one by one.
可以一個一個數的東西
4 dumplings · 4 顆水餃 2 apples · 2 顆蘋果 5 cookies · 5 片餅乾
How Much?
Uncountable
Things that are "masses" or liquids.
液體、粉末、無法一一數的東西
Soup · 湯 Shaved ice · 剉冰 Salt · 鹽 Pepper · 胡椒 Garlic · 蒜頭
IV.

My Favorite Snack

我最愛的小吃 · 用五感寫一段
Describe Your Favorite Snack
挑一樣你最愛的小吃，用五感各寫一句話。
🍢
Snack Name料理名稱
e.g. Stinky Tofu / 臭豆腐
👁️
It looks…看起來
It looks golden, crispy, and steaming.
👂
It sounds…聽起來
It sounds sizzling when it hits the oil.
👃
It smells…聞起來
It smells pungent — but in a good way!
✋
It feels…摸起來
It feels crispy outside and soft inside.
😋
It tastes…吃起來
It tastes salty, savory, and a little spicy.
