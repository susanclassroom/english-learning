The Ultimate Vibe Check
用 8 種情緒搭配音樂氛圍練習口說
I.

Today's Riddle

今日謎題
🌦️
"I change like the weather. Sometimes I am a sunny day, and sometimes I am a dark storm. You cannot touch me, but you can feel me. What am I?"
💭 A Mood
心情 · 情緒
A mood (情緒) changes like the weather — bright one moment, stormy the next. You can't touch it, but you can feel it. That's exactly what we're learning to describe today.

III.

Two Ways to Say It

兩種口說句型 · 描述音樂帶來的感受
STRUCTURE 01
The music is [Vibe], so I feel [Mood] because [Reason].
"The music is upbeat, so I feel hyped because I love dancing."
"The music is calm, so I feel peaceful because I can relax."
STRUCTURE 02
I feel / I'm [Mood] because the music is [Vibe].
"I feel nostalgic because this song is so dreamy."
"I'm bored because this music is so slow."
