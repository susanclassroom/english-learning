II.

Essential Vocabulary

必備單字（點 🔊 聽發音）

Taste & Texture · 味覺與質地

🍖
Savory
/ˈseɪ.vɚ.i/ · adj.
鹹香的 · 有肉味的 · 味道濃
Salty, meaty, or full of flavor.
"The miso soup is very savory."
🍋
Tangy
/ˈtæŋ.i/ · adj.
酸爽的 · 清新的（帶柑橘味）
Sharp, fresh, or citrus flavor.
"The cucumber salad is crunchy and tangy."
🌿
Fragrant
/ˈfreɪ.ɡrənt/ · adj.
香氣四溢的 · 芬芳的
Having a pleasant, sweet, or herbal smell.
"Alishan oolong tea is fragrant and light."
🥩
Tender
/ˈtɛn.dɚ/ · adj.
柔嫩的 · 好咬的（多形容肉）
Soft and easy to chew (usually for meat).
"The beef in the noodle soup is very tender."
🍲
Hearty
/ˈhɑːr.ti/ · adj.
豐盛的 · 飽足的
Large, warm, and very filling.
"I am looking for something hearty tonight."
🥨
Crunchy / Crispy
/ˈkrʌn.tʃi/ · /ˈkrɪs.pi/ · adj.
酥脆的 · 嚼起來有聲響
Firm and makes a sound when eaten.
"The garlic knots are buttery and crunchy."

Restaurant Terms · 餐廳用語

🧑
Diner
/ˈdaɪ.nɚ/ · n.
用餐的客人
The customer.
"The diner ordered the daily special."
🧑‍🍳
Waiter / Server
/ˈweɪ.tɚ/ · /ˈsɝː.vɚ/ · n.
服務生（為客人點餐的人）
The person taking the orders.
"The waiter brought us complimentary water."
🍽️
Main Course
/meɪn kɔːrs/ · n.
主菜
The primary dish of the meal.
"I'll have the Beef Noodle Soup as my main course."
🥗
Side Dish
/saɪd dɪʃ/ · n.
配菜（主菜之外的小份料理）
A smaller portion of food served with the main course.
"Would you like a side dish with that?"
📋
Daily Special
/ˈdeɪ.li ˈspeʃ.əl/ · n.
今日特餐（限今天供應）
A dish that is only available today.
"Our daily special is the Premium Sushi Set."
🎁
Complimentary
/ˌkɒm.plɪˈmen.tər.i/ · adj.
免費招待的（如水、麵包）
Something provided for free (like bread or water).
"Is the water complimentary? — Yes, it is!"

IV.

Roleplay Scripts

三個情境腳本 · 點 🔊 聽逐句範讀
SCENARIO 01
The Standard Order
Goal: Use a descriptive adjective to order a specific dish.
用一個形容詞，自信地點主菜。
Waiter
Welcome to our global food court! Are you ready to order?
Diner
Yes! I'm looking for something hearty. I'll have the Main Course Beef Noodles.
Waiter
Excellent. It is very savory today. Would you like a Side Dish with that?
Diner
Yes, please. I'll take the Spring Rolls.
Waiter
Great. Coming right up!
SCENARIO 02
The Sold-Out Dish
Goal: Use adjectives to describe an alternative dish.
當主菜賣完，用形容詞推薦別的料理。
Waiter
I'm very sorry, but we are actually out of the Pad Thai. It was very popular today.
Diner
Oh, that's too bad! What else would you recommend?
Waiter
Our Chicken Tikka Masala is a wonderful Main Course. It's very fragrant and creamy.
Diner
That sounds perfect. I'll try that instead. Is the water complimentary?
Waiter
Yes, it is! I'll bring some over immediately.
SCENARIO 03
The Honest Complaint
Goal: Identify a specific problem using texture/taste adjectives.
用質地與味覺的形容詞，禮貌地表達料理的問題。
Diner
Excuse me! I have a small problem with my Pizza.
Waiter
I am so sorry. What seems to be the matter?
Diner
It isn't very tender—it's actually quite tough to eat. Also, the crust isn't crunchy at all.
Waiter
I apologize for that. It should be perfectly crispy. Let me take this back to the kitchen.
Diner
Thank you. I appreciate the help.
Waiter
Of course. I will also bring a complimentary dessert for the wait.
