II.

A Quiet Specialty

陽明的另一條安靜傳統

Yangming has long been recognized for its wushu team — including the recent gold medal at the 2026 World Junior Championships in Tianjin. What is less talked about, but no less serious, is Yangming's taekwondo program. The two share a building, a discipline, and most of a coaching philosophy: show up early, respect each other, and trust the slow build.

陽明國中以武術隊聞名——包括最近在中國天津2026 世界青少年錦標賽奪下太極金牌。比較少被談到、但同樣認真的，是陽明的跆拳道校隊。兩支隊伍共用同一個訓練場、同一個守則、同一套教練哲學：早到、互相尊重、相信慢工的力量。

On March 17, 2026, results came in from the 2026 Taichung City Cup Taekwondo Open, one of central Taiwan's most competitive open tournaments. Yangming's athletes brought back three golds, two silvers, and eight bronzes — thirteen medals from a single weekend.

2026 年 3 月 17 日，2026 台中城市盃跆拳道公開賽成績揭曉。這是中部最競爭的公開賽之一。陽明的選手們從一個週末帶回3 金 2 銀 8 銅——十三面獎牌。

"Discipline, on a Saturday, with no one watching."

「禮拜六的紀律，沒有人在看時的紀律。」
